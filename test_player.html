Theater Everywhere — Test Page

Use this page to test the key bindings and theater mode styling inside your browser viewport.

How to test:

  1. Start playing the video below.
  2. Press the T key on your keyboard to expand the video player to fill the browser window.
  3. Press the T key again or the Escape key to restore the standard layout.
  4. Try toggling the extension on/off for this page via the toolbar popup and manage settings.
