Theater Everywhere — Multi-Player Test

Use this page to test viewport-aware activation and cycling when multiple players are present.

Testing Scenarios:

  1. Viewport Activation: Scroll to any individual video, play or hover it, and press T. The extension should maximize the video in the viewport.
  2. Multiple Players in View: Scroll to the bottom where there are two videos side-by-side.
    • Pressing T should select the one closer to the center, playing, or hovered.
    • Press Shift+T to cycle between the visible players without exiting theater mode.
    • Alternatively, click the "Switch Video" button in the custom bottom controls toolbar.
